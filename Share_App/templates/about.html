About SharePlate

Welcome to SharePlate, a project built with passion and dedication. This platform is designed to bring people together and make sharing meals, ideas, and connections easier.

My name is Muhammed Ajmal, and I’m from Palakkad, Kerala. I created SharePlate as a way to explore web development and deliver a solution that feels both simple and impactful.

SharePlate isn’t just a project — it’s a step towards building meaningful applications that can grow and help communities connect better.
